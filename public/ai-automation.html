Missed follow-ups

EXAMPLE WORKFLOW

A connected five-step enquiry process

Each step has one clear purpose and remains understandable for the business team.

01

Customer enquiry received

A lead arrives through the website, WhatsApp or social media.

02

Lead information organised

Name, contact details, required service and source are recorded clearly.

03

Follow-up reminder created

The responsible person gets a prompt for the next response.

04

Customer response tracked

The current stage is updated so the enquiry is not forgotten.

05

Next action suggested

The team can decide whether to call, send details or close the enquiry.
